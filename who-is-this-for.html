Who it's for

Made for people who think in one script and share in another

Textezi is for people who want to keep their language but present it in an easier Roman form for chat, notes, captions, and everyday sharing.

Everyday messaging

Type or speak in the script you already use, then send a Roman version that is easier for your audience to read quickly.

Creators and marketers

Prepare captions, replies, and short-form text in Roman script without changing the language itself.

Cross-script communication

Useful when your audience understands the language but prefers reading it in Roman letters instead of the original script.
