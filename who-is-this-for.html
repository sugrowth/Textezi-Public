Who it's for

For people who know the language but prefer reading it in English letters

Textezi is useful when the language should stay the same, but the writing needs to be easier to scan, type, copy, or share with a wider audience.

Everyday messaging

Type or speak in the writing you already use, then send a version in English letters that is easier for your audience to read quickly.

Creators and community teams

Prepare captions, replies, prompts, and short-form posts in English letters without turning the original language into English.

Cross-writing communication

Useful when your audience understands the language but prefers reading it in English letters instead of the original writing.

A quick check

Textezi is a fit when these are true

The product works best when the reader already understands the language and only needs the writing to feel more familiar.

You want to keep the original language, not translate it.
Your audience reads English letters more comfortably than the native script.
