Use Cases

For people who speak the language but prefer Roman reading

Textezi fits situations where the language should stay intact but Roman text is easier for the audience to read, scan, or share.

Everyday messaging

Useful when people understand the language but expect it in Roman characters in chats, notes, or replies.

Creators and community teams

Helps prepare captions, comments, or audience-facing short-form text without translating away the original language.

Cross-script communication

Fits bilingual or cross-region situations where Roman script improves accessibility without changing the language itself.
