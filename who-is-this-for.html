Who it's for

Made for people who speak in one way and prefer reading it in English letters

Textezi is for people who want to keep their language but show it in English letters for chat, notes, captions, and everyday sharing.

Everyday messaging

Type or speak in the writing you already use, then send a version in English letters that is easier for your audience to read quickly.

Creators and marketers

Prepare captions, replies, and short-form text in English letters without changing the language itself.

Cross-writing communication

Useful when your audience understands the language but prefers reading it in English letters instead of the original writing.
