Frequently asked questions

Practical answers about what Textezi does, what it does not do, and how the product is meant to work.

Features & Pricing
Textezi converts supported scripts into easy Roman writing while keeping the same language. It changes how text is written, not what it means.
The current product direction is 20 hours of active usage first, then a one-time permanent unlock at the lowest Google Play-allowed local price.
Languages & Conversion
Hindi and Punjabi are the launch languages. More languages can be added later, but only enabled and supported policies should convert.
No. Textezi is about romanization, not translation. Hindi stays Hindi and Punjabi stays Punjabi. Only the script changes.
Automation
No. Textezi is designed to work with the user's existing keyboard workflow rather than replacing the keyboard.
The floating bubble is the planned quick control for minimized mode. It should let users reopen Textezi, pause automation, or resume quickly.
Privacy & Support
The product direction is privacy-first and on-device for the core romanization flow. Text and preferences should remain local to the phone.
