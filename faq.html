FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Straight answers about what Textezi does today, what it does not do, and how the product fits into normal Android use.

Features & Pricing
Textezi takes supported text and rewrites it in English letters while keeping the same language. It changes how text is written, not what it means.
Textezi includes a soft 30-day trial on the device, followed by a one-time permanent unlock through Google Play. Users can unlock early at any time.
Languages & Conversion
Textezi is live today with Hindi and Punjabi. The app can show other language options in Settings, but only fully supported languages that you enable are allowed to convert.
No. Textezi does not turn Hindi into English or Punjabi into English. It keeps the same language and only changes how it is written. You can think of it as writing your language in English letters.
Automation
No. Textezi is designed to work with your existing keyboard workflow rather than replacing it with a custom keyboard.
The floating bubble is an optional quick control. When enabled, it can reopen Textezi faster and make it easier to pause, resume, or switch the automation behavior while using other apps.
Privacy & Support
The main conversion flow is designed to stay on the device. Textezi is built to avoid unnecessary handling of your text outside the phone for normal use.
Use the support page or open an issue directly here: sugrowth/Textezi-Public/issues/new.
