Terms

Simple terms for using Textezi

These terms explain what Textezi does, how access works, and what to expect from the product and support pages.

What the product does
Textezi helps people show supported text in English letters while keeping the same language.
It changes how text is written, not what the text means.
It is not a translation app and it is not sold as a replacement keyboard.
Billing and access
Textezi currently uses a soft 30-day trial followed by a one-time core unlock through Google Play.
Optional AI assist, when available, is separate from the core unlock and may require a monthly Google Play subscription.
Pricing, availability, and billing depend on the current app release and Google Play support.
Google Play purchase terms also apply where purchases are used.
Availability and updates
Features and language support can change as the product improves over time.
The app labels language support as Validated, Baseline, or Planned so users can tell focused quality coverage from early local coverage.
Only languages that are available and enabled in the current app release should be treated as live product support.
Some workflows depend on Android permissions, Google Play billing availability, or device support.
Ownership and support
Textezi, its branding, and its site content remain the property of Sugrowth.
Public help pages and support links exist to explain the product and collect feedback.
