Terms

Terms for the app, public site, and repository

These notes describe the current public scope of Textezi, the support repository, and the permission-based Android features that make the app work.

Product scope
Textezi is a script-conversion product. It is intended to preserve the language and change only the script into Roman output for supported languages.
Launch support is intentionally narrower than the broader language list visible in the app.
Unsupported or not-yet-live languages may remain unchanged by design even if they appear in Settings.
Permissions and feature prerequisites
Accessibility automation requires user consent and the relevant Android accessibility permission.
Suggest only mode and the floating bubble may also require display-over-other-apps permission.
Without the required permissions, Textezi may fall back to manual, share, or Process Text workflows instead of in-place automation.
Purchases and access
The app currently describes access as a soft 30-day trial followed by a one-time permanent unlock through Google Play.
Purchase availability, pricing labels, and billing readiness depend on Google Play configuration and the release state of the app.
Google Play purchases and account handling are also subject to Google Play’s own terms and policies.
Ownership and support
The app, public site, branding, and public content are protected by copyright.
The public repository is intended for website content, public product documentation, and issue tracking.
Support requests, bug reports, and feature suggestions should go through the Textezi-Public GitHub repository.
