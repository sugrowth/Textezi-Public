Features

Built to make text easier to read and share

Textezi is shaped around one job: keep the language the same, show it in English letters, and let the user stay in control of what changes and what does not.

Same words, different writing

Textezi changes how text is written, not what it means. It helps you keep your language while showing it in English letters.

General by design

Textezi is built as a broader selected-language system, with support expanding carefully over time instead of trying to force every language at once.

Keep your keyboard

Textezi is designed around the user's existing typing and dictation workflow instead of replacing the keyboard.

Copy and share

The converted text is meant to be easy to copy, paste, and share into other apps.

On-device by default

The product direction keeps the core text-conversion flow on-device rather than pushing user text to a backend.

Automation controls

Suggest only, auto mode, floating bubble control, and manual fallback paths are there so the workflow stays flexible instead of forced.
