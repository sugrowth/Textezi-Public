About

Why Textezi exists

Many people speak the language they want to write, but read it faster in English letters. Textezi, pronounced Text Easy, is built for that everyday use case.

What it does

The same language, easier to read and share

Textezi is not a translation app. It keeps the same words and changes only the writing, so supported text can be shown in English letters without changing the meaning.

Keep the language. Change only the writing.
What makes it useful

Built for real writing habits

Textezi is meant to fit the way people already write on Android instead of forcing a new keyboard, a new language, or a complicated workflow.

Same meaning

Textezi changes how supported text is written, not what it means.

Normal Android flow

Use the app directly, share text in, or use Android text actions without replacing your keyboard.

Private by default

The main writing-conversion flow is designed to stay on your device.
