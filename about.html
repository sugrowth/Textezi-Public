About Textezi

Textezi is a Sugrowth product built to make it easier to keep the same language while writing it in English letters.

Our promise

Keep the language. Write it in English letters.

Textezi is not a translation tool. It keeps the same words and changes only how they are written, so text becomes easier to read, copy, and send.

Private by design. No custom keyboard. Built to make language easier to read and share.
The company

Sugrowth

Sugrowth builds products focused on meaningful personal and practical growth. Textezi fits that mission by helping people use language in the way they naturally prefer to read and write.
