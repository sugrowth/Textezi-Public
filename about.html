About

Romanization built around real Android use

Textezi is a mobile-first product for people who want to keep the language the same and change only how it is written. The app is designed to fit normal Android text workflows instead of asking users to adopt a custom keyboard or a translation-heavy product model.

Our Promise

Simple, private, and specific

Use your normal keyboard, speak or paste in your language, and get Roman output that is easier to read, copy, and send. Textezi only changes the languages you enable and leaves everything else alone.

Keep the language. Change the script. Keep the workflow in your hands.
What It Is Built Around

The app’s product principles

Same language, different script

Textezi preserves the language and rewrites only the script into easier Latin letters for people who read that form faster.

Android-first workflow

Manual conversion, sharing into the app, Process Text, and accessibility automation all exist so the product can meet real Android use cases.

Local correction memory

Saved corrections and reusable preferred spellings stay on the device instead of depending on a cloud-first core experience.
