# Textezi Privacy

Textezi keeps core script-conversion workflows local-first.

- Core conversion, Auto mode, and Suggest only are designed to stay on the phone
- Preferences, enabled languages, automation settings, and local dictionary corrections remain local for the core workflow
- Optional AI assist is separate; when the user chooses Refine with AI or AI mode, only the text needed for that AI refinement request may be sent to the configured AI provider
- Google Play handles one-time unlock and optional AI subscription purchase processing
- The public repository exists for website pages, issues, and public documentation

The public HTML privacy page lives at `privacy.html`.
