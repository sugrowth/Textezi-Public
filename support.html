Support

Support for real product use, not guesswork

If conversion breaks, automation behaves oddly, or a language is missing, the fastest way to get help is to report the exact app flow and a safe example.

Best report includes
Where it happened Tell us whether the issue was in the converter, share flow, Process Text, Auto, or Suggest only.
Language setup Include the enabled languages and their priority order when it matters.
Device details Android version, device model, and app version help narrow it down quickly.
Safe example A short non-sensitive sample or screenshot is usually the fastest path to a fix.

Report a product bug

Open an issue if romanization, accessibility automation, billing, or a specific Android flow is behaving incorrectly.

Request a language or workflow improvement

Suggest new language support, automation refinements, copy improvements, or better Android workflow coverage.

Review known issues

See current feedback, fixes, and public discussion around launch scope, bugs, and edge cases.
Helpful reports

What to include when something breaks

The best support requests describe the product state around the bug, not just the symptom you saw on screen.

Your Android version, device model, and the app version or build if visible.
Whether the issue happened in the home converter, Android share flow, Process Text, Auto mode, or Suggest only.
Which languages were selected, and whether the floating bubble or accessibility automation was enabled.
A safe sample input, screenshot, or screen recording when the text is not sensitive.
