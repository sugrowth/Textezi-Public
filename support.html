Helpful Reports

What to include when something breaks

The faster way to get a useful answer is to describe the actual app state around the issue, not just the symptom.

Your Android version, device model, and the app version or build if visible.
Whether the issue happened in the home converter, Android share flow, Process Text, Auto mode, or Suggest only.
Which languages were selected, and whether the floating bubble or accessibility automation was enabled.
A safe sample input, screenshot, or screen recording when the text is not sensitive.
