Privacy

Your text should stay yours

Textezi is built so core conversion works on your device. Optional AI assist is separate and only runs when you choose an AI action or AI mode.

On-device conversion
Core conversion, Auto mode, and Suggest only are designed to run on your device.
Textezi changes supported text into English letters without needing to change the meaning of the message.
Your selected languages and saved corrections are meant to stay local to the device.
Permissions
Accessibility automation is optional and must be enabled by the user before Textezi can act in other editable fields.
Some features depend on Android permissions, such as accessibility access or showing controls over other apps.
If you do not enable them, you can still use Textezi through the normal in-app and copy/share flow.
Protected fields
Textezi is not supposed to rewrite passwords, OTPs, or other account-sensitive fields.
The product is designed to stay away from fields that should never be changed automatically.
That boundary is part of making the product safe to use in everyday apps.
Optional AI assist
AI assist is separate from core conversion and from normal Auto / Suggest only automation.
If you choose Refine with AI or Accessibility AI mode, Textezi can send only the text needed for that AI refinement request to the configured AI provider.
AI assist may be limited by request size, daily requests, monthly words, and monthly subscription status.
Payments and support
The app uses a soft 30-day trial and a one-time unlock through Google Play.
Optional AI assist, when available, is handled as a separate monthly subscription through Google Play.
Purchase handling is managed by Google Play where billing is available.
Support requests go through the Textezi support links and issue form, not through your converted text itself.
Plain-language summary

What this means in practice

Textezi is meant to help you write in a form that is easier to read, while keeping the product narrow and careful about where it acts.

It is not a custom keyboard.
It is not a translation app.
It is meant to keep core conversion, Auto mode, and Suggest only on your device.
AI assist is optional, explicit, and separate from the local core workflow.
It should stay away from sensitive fields and account-related text.
